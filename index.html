RunEarn Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

RunEarn helps users manage income, expenses, and financial reports. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user data.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as email address and Google sign-in information when users create an account or log in. We may also store transaction-related data such as income, expense, category, date, and description entered by the user.

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide app features including authentication, transaction tracking, financial reports, and account access.

Third-Party Services

RunEarn may use Firebase services including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. These services are provided by Google Firebase.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user personal data. User data may be processed by third-party services required to operate the app, such as Firebase.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

RunEarn may be used by younger users with the guidance or permission of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review or delete the information where required by law.

Parental Control

Parents or guardians may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of their child's personal information.
