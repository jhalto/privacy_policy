RunEarn Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: June 2026

This page explains how RunEarn users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

Contact email: w3aiweb@gmail.com

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your RunEarn account, please send an email to: w3aiweb@gmail.com

Use the email subject: RunEarn Account Deletion Request

In your email, include the email address used for your RunEarn account. This helps us verify and locate your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

After your request is verified, we will delete your RunEarn account and associated data, including:

  • Account information, such as email address or login identifier
  • Saved income records
  • Saved expense records
  • Transaction categories
  • Transaction dates and descriptions
  • Financial report data generated from your transactions

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or backup recovery.

Processing Time

We aim to process verified account deletion requests within 30 days.

Guest Users

If you use RunEarn as a guest, uninstalling the app or clearing app data may remove locally stored guest data from your device. If any guest data is stored on our servers, you may contact us for deletion assistance.

Request Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete only some of your data without deleting your full account, you can contact us at zobayerarmannadim@gmail.com. We will review your request and assist where possible.

Contact

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact us at: zobayerarmannadim@gmail.com
